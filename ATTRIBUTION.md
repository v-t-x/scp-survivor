## Project-generated visual assets / 项目生成视觉素材

The shipped pixel assets were generated and edited for this project without third-party image inputs according to the project asset register. The register records prompts, processing, dimensions, admission status, and remaining commercial-review risks.

根据项目素材登记，随项目交付的像素素材为本项目生成并编辑，未使用第三方图片输入。登记表记录了提示词、处理过程、尺寸、准入状态以及仍待商业复核的风险。

Under this local proposal, project-generated assets that are SCP-derived are included in the proposed SCP-derived CC BY-SA 3.0 candidate category. The final license for project-generated visual assets that are not SCP-derived remains unresolved, pending Level 2 and project-owner review before public distribution.

按本地候选方案，具有 SCP 衍生性质的项目生成素材归入拟议的 SCP 衍生 CC BY-SA 3.0 候选类别。不具有 SCP 衍生性质的项目生成视觉素材，其最终许可仍未确定，首次公开分发前须经 Level 2 与项目所有者复核。

详见 `docs/art/asset-register.md` 与 `docs/licensing-and-commercialization.md`。本文件用于记录项目候选署名，不构成法律意见。
